Girl with Red Hair & a Purple Shirt

About This Image

This was a digital painting I did a little over half a year ago to practice painting with colors and coloring over sketches. I personally really like the cat to the side of her, the real art in the image.

Why PNG Format?

Since this was a digital drawing I did, I felt best to use PNG format to keep the high quality of colors in the painting, especially the detailed eyes I painted. It also keeps the crunchiness of the sketch lines in the cat.
