About Us

Company Background History

PixelForge Studios is a small indie gaming studio dedicated to creating emotionally engaging and visually rich interactive experiences. Specializing in narrative-driven games, pixel-art worlds, and experimental mechanics, the studio's team includes artists, developers, composers, and writers who collaborate to deliver memorable gameplay. PixelForge Studios places a strong emphasis on accessibility features to ensure their games are inclusive.

Team Biographies

PixelForge Studios is comprised of a small, dedicated team of designers and developers who team together to produce timely, polished projects and updates. Bryant Henderson, the leader of our team of developers received his Masters in Software Engineering from New York Chicago Bulls University.
